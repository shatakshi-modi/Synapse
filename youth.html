Youth RUN

Youth run is a 5km run organized under the banner of Synapse with the aim to give the Youth a platform to speak about the social cause they are passionate about and give back to the community. Youth Run’19 organized in association with UNICEF India to promote the importance of nutrition. Youth Run’20 was organized in virtual mode on an application called FeetApart, abiding by the covid guidelines and social distancing.
